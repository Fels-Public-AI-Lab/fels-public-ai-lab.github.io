What We Do

Research

Investigating the intersection of policy and technology to understand how AI can better serve the public good while maintaining institutional integrity.

Teaching

Educating the next generation of public leaders on the ethical implementation and strategic oversight of artificial intelligence in government.

Civic Tech

Building open-source tools and agents that solve real-world problems for municipalities and public sector organizations.

Featured Project

Philadelphia Property Tax MCP

A Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that provides real-time access to Philadelphia's the OPA property data, enabling AI agents to analyze assessment history and tax balance data for civic advocacy.
