What We Do

Advising

Partnering directly with public sector organizations to assess AI readiness, identify high-value use cases, and navigate implementation risks. We bring academic rigor to real agency decisions.

Teaching

Preparing the next generation of public administrators to use, evaluate, and govern AI responsibly. Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on practice, ethical judgment, and practical deployment skills.

Building

Developing open-source tools and AI agents designed for the specific constraints of government work. Everything we build is publicly available and free to adapt for civic use.

Featured Project

Philadelphia Property Tax MCP

A Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that connects AI assistants directly to Philadelphia's Office of Property Assessment data — making live property records queryable in plain language for the first time.

  • Queries live OPA assessment data, including current valuations, exemptions, and multi-year assessment history for any Philadelphia parcel
  • Enables equity analysis by surfacing patterns in assessment changes across neighborhoods and property types
  • Supports appeal preparation by giving homeowners and advocates instant access to the data they need to challenge inaccurate assessments
