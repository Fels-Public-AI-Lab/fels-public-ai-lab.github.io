Public Projects

Working in the open to build tools that matter.

The Fels Public AI Lab builds open-source tools, datasets, and AI configurations that make government data genuinely accessible. Our projects span civic technology, public finance education, and AI tools for government practitioners. All work is openly licensed and built in public.

Civic Data Access

Building AI interfaces to public government data systems.

Philadelphia Property Tax MCP Server

Active

Connects AI assistants to Philadelphia's property assessment and tax balance data through the city's public CARTO API. Enables natural language queries over property records — supporting equity research, appeal preparation, and civic transparency.

For: Researchers, civic technologists, property owners

Public Finance Education

Interactive tools for teaching public budgeting and financial management.

Sales Tax Revenue Forecasting

Active

A browser-based simulation for forecasting sales tax revenues using historical trends and economic assumptions. Students work through projection scenarios used in real municipal budget processes.

For: MPA students and instructors

Revenue Forecasting Education
Open Exercise GitHub

Budget Trend Analysis

Active

An interactive exercise in analyzing multi-year budget data to identify spending trends, variance patterns, and fiscal pressures. Builds skills in reading and interpreting government financial reports.

For: MPA students and instructors

Budget Analysis Education
Open Exercise GitHub

Personnel Budget

Active

A hands-on exercise in building and managing a personnel budget — the largest cost driver in most public agencies. Covers position costing, salary scheduling, and benefits calculations.

For: MPA students and instructors

Personnel Costs Education
Open Exercise GitHub

Nonprofit Cash Flow & Liquidity

Active

A simulation-based exercise exploring how a grant-dependent nonprofit can be financially healthy on paper while facing real cash crises mid-year. Students build a monthly cash flow model, calculate liquidity ratios, and stress-test the organization against delayed grant disbursements.

For: MPA students and instructors

Nonprofit Finance Cash Flow Education
Open Exercise GitHub

Capital Fund Planning

Active

A full-cycle capital budgeting exercise modeled on a major regional transit authority. Students build a 5-year Capital Improvement Plan, allocate funding across federal, state, and local sources, construct a 20-year bond amortization schedule, and assess the operating budget impact of debt service.

For: MPA students and instructors
